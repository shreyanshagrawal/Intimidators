       HPCL Intel – AI-Powered Sales Intelligence Platform
HPCL Intel is an AI-driven sales intelligence and lead prioritization system
designed for large energy enterprises to identify, qualify, and convert
high-value industrial leads using public signals, tenders, and market data.

      Product Vision – HPCL Intel
## Problem
Sales officers rely on manual tracking of tenders and delayed information,
leading to missed high-value opportunities.

## Solution
HPCL Intel converts fragmented public signals into actionable intelligence
with confidence scoring and explainability.

## Why It Wins
- Signal-driven, not CRM-driven
- Explainable AI (not black box)
- Designed for PSU workflows
- Officer-first UX

## Long-Term Vision
- Predictive demand forecasting
- Competitor activity tracking
- Auto-generated outreach content

       Key Capabilities
- Signal-based lead detection (tenders, expansion news, ops signals)
- AI-powered product recommendations
- Sales conversion probability scoring
- Smart action workflows for field officers
- Explainable intelligence (why a lead is hot)
